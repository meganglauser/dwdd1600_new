About

Hello,my name is Megan Glauser, and I am a self taught artist from Arizona. I have loved anything creative ever since I can remember and as I got older my love for painting grew.It is my goal to capture the world around me through a creative lesnse seeing beauty in the small things. Life can get so busy that we often forget to take a step back to notice the beauty in nature all around us, I hope viewing some of my artwork helps remind you to slow things down and take in nature beauty.
